## Key Stats to Consider When Choosing a Weapon

When selecting a weapon in Pixel Blade, several key stats play a significant role in your performance. Here are the most important stats to consider, as highlighted in the **Pixel Blade Wiki weapons**:

- **Damage**: The amount of damage the weapon can deal in one hit. This is a primary factor in choosing the best weapon for heavy combat.
- **Attack Speed**: Determines how quickly you can strike with the weapon. Fast attack speed is essential for dealing with multiple enemies in rapid succession.
- **Range**: For ranged weapons like bows and magic wands, the range determines how far your attacks can reach.
- **Durability**: This stat affects how long your weapon will last before needing repairs or replacement.
- **Special Abilities**: Many weapons have unique abilities or effects, such as poison damage, elemental attacks, or life steal.

Each weapon in Pixel Blade is designed to cater to a specific playstyle, so it's important to balance these stats to suit your preferred approach. For instance, swords with high attack speed are great for fast-paced combat, while axes with higher damage are suited for tanking.
