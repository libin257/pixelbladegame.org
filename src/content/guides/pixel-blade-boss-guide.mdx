## Popular Pixel Blade Bosses and How to Beat Them

Here, we break down strategies for several of the most well-known bosses in Pixel Blade.

### 1. The Shadow King

The Shadow King is one of the first major bosses you'll encounter, but don't be fooled—his powers are formidable. He uses shadow magic that can drain your health quickly.

- **Weakness**: Light-based attacks.
- **Strategy**: Stay mobile and keep an eye on his telegraphed shadow attacks. Using light-based spells or weapons will deal extra damage. Additionally, make sure to avoid the dark vortex he summons, which can trap you in one spot.

### 2. The Infernal Beast

This fiery beast is a challenge even for experienced players. Its fire-based attacks deal massive damage, and its rage phase can overwhelm you.

- **Weakness**: Ice or water-based attacks.
- **Strategy**: Keep your distance and use ice-based spells or weapons. The key is to time your attacks and retreat when the boss enters its rage mode. Always be ready to dodge its fireball barrage.

### 3. The Arcane Warden

The Arcane Warden has a set of magical attacks that deal devastating damage if you’re not quick enough to dodge.

- **Weakness**: Dispel or magic-nullifying attacks.
- **Strategy**: Focus on staying behind cover to avoid the Warden’s magic projectiles. When the Warden casts its protective spell, use dispel magic to counter its shield and deal massive damage.
