## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How often does the pixel blade tier list change?
The **pixel blade tier list** receives minor adjustments monthly with major updates, and significant shifts quarterly with large content patches. We update this page within 48 hours of any balance changes.

### 2. Should I use lower-tier weapons if I enjoy their playstyle?
Absolutely! This **pixel blade tier list** reflects optimal performance, but player skill and enjoyment matter more. B-tier weapons in skilled hands can outperform S-tier weapons used poorly.

### 3. What's the best weapon for beginners in the pixel blade tier list?
For beginners, focus on A-tier weapons with high versatility like Thunderstrike or Bloodthirster. They're easier to obtain than S+ tier weapons while still performing well in all content.

### 4. How does PvP change the pixel blade tier list rankings?
PvP heavily favors burst damage and mobility, making Shadow Fang and Stellar Blade even more dominant. Sustained DPS weapons like Void Reaver drop slightly in PvP-specific rankings.
